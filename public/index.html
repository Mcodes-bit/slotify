How to get reminders when the website is closed:

To ensure you never miss a deadline even when the app is closed, you can use the Calendar Export feature we built:

  1. Go to the Calendar tab in Slotify.
  2. Click Export to Google / Apple Calendar (.ics).
  3. Import this file into your calendar app (Google Calendar, Outlook, or Apple Calendar).
  4. Since these calendar apps are integrated into your operating system (Windows/macOS) or phone, they will trigger native system notifications and alarms on your desktop or mobile device, even if your browser is completely shut down!
